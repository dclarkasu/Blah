    United States Marine Corps

    Daniel joined the Marine Corps right after college and completed basic training in Parris Island, SC and the School of Infantry at Camp LeJeune, NC. He served as an Infantry Scout with 1st Light Armor Reconnaissance Battalion out of Camp Pendleton, CA. Beginning as a Rifleman, he advanced through the positions of Scout Team Leader, Section Leader, and eventually Squad Leader commanding 12 Marines. Daniel was deployed around the world for the majority of 2015 with the 15th Marine Expeditionary Unit. He was honorably discharged in 2017 as a Corporal and, immediately following his release from Active Duty, was promoted to Sergeant.
